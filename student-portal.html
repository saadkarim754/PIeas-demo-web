Policy for Cheating Cases

PIEAS Academic Integrity and Cheating Prevention Policy

Academic Integrity Policy

1. Definition of Academic Dishonesty

Academic dishonesty includes plagiarism, cheating on examinations, unauthorized collaboration, and fabrication of data.

2. Consequences

  • First Offense: Warning and zero marks in that assignment/exam
  • Second Offense: Suspension from courses and academic probation
  • Third Offense: Dismissal from the university

3. Reporting Violations

Students witnessing academic dishonesty should report to their course instructor or the academic office immediately.

4. Appeals Process

Students have the right to appeal any disciplinary decision within 15 days of notification.
